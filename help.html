❓ LockCMD Help Guide Locksmith Command Center — guided intake flows for locksmiths

LockCMD helps your customers tell you what they need without a long phone call. They answer a few simple questions, then their phone opens a text or email already filled in. They just tap send, and you get a clean summary.

There are four flows, one for each type of locksmith work.

⚙️ Setup Guide — First Time

The first time you open LockCMD on a new device, you'll see the setup wizard. This is where you configure the app for your business.

🔧
  1. Welcome screen — click "Get Started" to begin, or "Skip" to use defaults
  2. Business name — this appears in the top bar so customers know who they're contacting
  3. Which services? — toggle the tabs you offer. Turn off anything you don't do. Your customers will only see the tabs you leave on.
  4. Brand colors — match LockCMD to your website. Pick an accent color, secondary color, and background. Or keep the defaults.
  5. Logo & background — optional. Paste a URL to your logo image or a background image.
  6. Your contact info — your phone number and email go into the SMS/email links at the end of each flow. If you enter your number as +15551234567, the text will be pre-addressed to you.
  7. Create a PIN — this locks the settings page so customers can't change your configuration. 4-6 digits. Don't forget it.
  8. Done! — LockCMD launches with your settings applied.
Example: Bob is a locksmith who only does residential and safe work. In step 3 he turns off Commercial and Automotive. Then he sets his accent color to match his blue website, enters his phone number as +15556789012, and sets PIN 4829. Now when customers open LockCMD, they only see Residential and Safe tabs, and the text links go straight to Bob's phone.

🔒 Changing Settings Later

After setup, the ⚙️ gear icon appears in the top bar. Click it, enter your PIN, and you'll get the full settings panel where you can:

  • Show/hide any tab
  • Change business name, phone, or email
  • Change colors, logo, background
  • Change your PIN
  • Export settings to a .json file for backup
  • Import settings from a previous backup
  • Reset All — wipes everything back to defaults (including PIN)
Example: Bob decides to add Commercial service later. He clicks ⚙️, enters PIN 4829, toggles Commercial back on, and clicks Done. No setup wizard needed — it's live immediately.

🏠 Residential Flow

For homeowners needing lockout help, rekeying, repairs, or new locks.

🔑
  1. Pick the situation — locked out, rekey, repair, new install, or key duplication
  2. Pick the lock type — deadbolt, knob, handle set, smart lock, or not sure
  3. Set urgency — emergency (locked out now), today, or schedule
  4. Enter your name
  5. Enter your phone number
  6. Enter the service address
Example: Sarah is locked out of her house at 7pm. She picks "Locked out" → "Deadbolt" → "Emergency" → types her name, phone, and address. Her phone opens a text that says "RESIDENTIAL LOCKSMITH REQUEST — Sarah, locked out, deadbolt, EMERGENCY, 123 Main St." She taps send. You get the text and call her back or dispatch.

🏢 Commercial Flow

For businesses needing master key systems, rekeying, panic hardware, or access control.

🏢
  1. Pick the business type — office, warehouse, retail, medical, school, other
  2. Pick the service — master key, rekey, panic hardware, new install, access control, or consultation
  3. Approx number of doors — 1-5, 6-15, 16-30, or 30+
  4. Enter company name
  5. Enter phone number
  6. Enter business address
Example: A property manager needs a master key system for an 8-unit building. He picks "Office" → "Master key system" → "6-15 doors" → enters company info. His phone opens a text with everything filled in. He sends it to you. You have the details before you even pick up the phone.

🚗 Automotive Flow

For car lockouts, lost keys, broken ignition, or fob replacement.

🚗
  1. Pick the situation — locked out, lost keys, broken key, new fob, trunk locked, other
  2. Enter vehicle year
  3. Enter vehicle make & model
  4. Pick the location type — home, parking lot, store, street, other
  5. Enter your name
  6. Enter your phone number
Example: Tom locked his keys in a 2021 Ford F-150 at the grocery store. He picks "Locked out" → enters "2021" → "Ford F-150" → "Store parking" → name and phone. His phone opens a text: "AUTOMOTIVE SERVICE REQUEST — Tom, 2021 Ford F-150, locked out, store parking." He sends it to you. You know exactly what tools to bring.

🔒 Safe Flow

For customers needing safe opening, combo changes, repairs, or restoration.

🔒
  1. Pick the safe type — home/gun, commercial, high-security, antique, floor/wall, jewelry chest
  2. Pick the issue — won't open, dial spins, keypad dead, combo forgotten, handle broken, won't lock
  3. Enter brand (if known — AMSEC, Gardall, Liberty, etc.)
  4. Pick approximate age — new, modern, vintage, antique, or unknown
  5. Enter your name
  6. Enter your phone number
Example: A customer has an antique floor safe with a combo they forgot. They pick "Floor/wall safe" → "Combo forgotten" → enter "Sargent & Greenleaf" → "Antique (50+)" → name and phone. You get a text: "SAFE SERVICE REQUEST — Antique floor safe, Sargent & Greenleaf, combo forgotten." You know it's a vintage dial job before you even call them back.

📱 How to Send

At the end of any flow, the customer sees two options:

  • 📱 Send via Text — opens their messaging app with the message already written. They just tap send.
  • 📧 Send via Email — opens their email app with the subject filled in. They just tap send.

The locksmith's contact info is already in the message so the customer doesn't need to look anything up.

❓ Tips

  • Each flow takes about 30 seconds to complete
  • Customers can go back with the ← Back button if they make a mistake
  • They can start over with the "Start Over" button at the end
  • Switch between tabs anytime — each flow is independent
  • Perfect for elderly customers who struggle with texting — the SMS link does all the work
